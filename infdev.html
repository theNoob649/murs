Minecraft Infdev

Release Date: February 27, 2010

Infdev was a game changer, introducing infinite world generation—meaning the map would stretch endlessly, letting players explore far beyond previous limits.

This version added caves, ores, new biomes, and redstone mechanics, setting the stage for more complex gameplay and creativity.

  • Infinite world generation for limitless exploration
  • New caves, ores, and biomes
  • Introduction of redstone mechanics for basic circuits
  • Improved mobs and more complex terrain
