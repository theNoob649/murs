Minecraft 1.9: The Combat Update

Release Date: February 29, 2016

The Combat Update overhauled Minecraft’s battle mechanics. Cooldowns were added to weapons, making spamming attacks ineffective. Shields replaced sword blocking, dual-wielding became possible, and new arrows introduced potion effects to ranged combat.

But the most iconic change? The End itself.

  • The Ender Dragon fight was redesigned with iron bars protecting healing crystals and more varied attack phases.
  • Players can now respawn the Ender Dragon by crafting and placing End Crystals on the exit portal.
  • End Cities and End Ships spawned after the dragon’s defeat, containing Elytra and valuable loot.

1.9 didn’t just change combat—it gave players a reason to return to The End.
