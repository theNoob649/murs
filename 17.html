Minecraft 1.17: Caves & Cliffs Part I

Release Date: June 8, 2021

The first part of the Caves & Cliffs update brought exciting new ores like copper, amethyst geodes, and the deep dark biome teaser with sculk sensors. It laid the foundation for transforming underground exploration.

Tiny addition:

Spyglass was added quietly, letting players zoom in on distant objects and giving exploration a fresh tactical edge.
