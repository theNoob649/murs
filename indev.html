Minecraft Indev

Release Date: December 23, 2009

Indev marked Minecraft’s first step towards survival gameplay. It introduced health and hunger, crafting basics, and hostile mobs like zombies, bringing tension and challenge to the blocky world.

This phase also began experimenting with terrain generation, although the world size was still limited.

  • Added survival mechanics: health, hunger, and crafting
  • First hostile mobs like zombies
  • Early terrain generation with limited map size
  • Introduction of basic tools and weapons
