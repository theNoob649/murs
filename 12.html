Minecraft 1.12: World of Color Update

Release Date: June 7, 2017

The World of Color Update revamped Minecraft’s color palette and creative potential.

Key features included:

  • Concrete & Concrete Powder: Bright, solid-colored blocks perfect for builds.
  • Glazed Terracotta: Patterned decorative blocks with unique directional designs.
  • Parrots: A new jungle-dwelling mob that mimics nearby hostile sounds.
  • Advancements: A redesigned achievement system that guided players through gameplay goals.

This update focused heavily on creativity and visual aesthetics, helping builders create more colorful and stylish structures.
