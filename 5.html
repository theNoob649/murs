Minecraft 1.5: The Redstone Update

Release Date: March 13, 2013

The Redstone Update supercharged Minecraft’s engineering possibilities with new blocks and mechanics.

  • Hoppers: Automated item transfer systems for sorting and storage.
  • Comparators: New redstone component for complex circuits and signal strength measurement.
  • Trapped Chests: Chests that emit a redstone signal when opened.
  • Daylight Sensors: Blocks that detect sunlight, enabling timed redstone contraptions.
  • Dropper: Similar to dispensers but simply drops items instead of using them.
  • Improved Performance: Lots of bug fixes and optimizations.

This update unlocked huge creative potential for players obsessed with automation and redstone builds.
