Read about Classic →

This is the Creative build from November 2009, preserved as it shipped. There is no health, no hunger, and no way to save — closing the tab ends the world.

What it does have is the thing that made Minecraft spread in the first place: an open world made of blocks you can take apart and put back however you like.
