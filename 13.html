Minecraft 1.13: Update Aquatic

Release Date: July 18, 2018

The Update Aquatic transformed Minecraft’s oceans into vibrant underwater worlds full of life and adventure.

Major additions included:

  • Drowned: A new underwater zombie variant.
  • Turtles: Passive mobs that lay eggs on beaches.
  • Dolphins: Helpful mobs that guide players to underwater treasures.
  • Phantoms: Flying hostile mobs that attack players who haven’t slept.

Players could explore shipwrecks, ocean ruins, and coral reefs, while using new items like the Trident, Heart of the Sea, and Conduits for underwater exploration and combat.

Swimming mechanics were also overhauled, making underwater movement smoother and faster.
