Minecraft Beta

Release Date: December 20, 2010

The Beta phase polished Minecraft’s survival experience and multiplayer support, adding villages, hunger mechanics, pistons, shears, and more.

It was the build-up to the full 1.0 release, setting the game up for official launch with a ton of refinements.

  • Added villages and village mechanics
  • Introduced hunger and better survival balancing
  • Added pistons, shears, and new blocks
  • Improved multiplayer features and stability
