Minecraft 1.6: The Horse Update

Release Date: July 1, 2013

The Horse Update introduced a bunch of new ways to travel and explore the world faster.

  • Horses, Donkeys, and Mules: Tamed and ridden, changing how players move around.
  • Horse Armor: Protect your new rides with iron, gold, and diamond armors.
  • Leads: Connect mobs and animals to keep them close or lead them somewhere.
  • Hay Bales: Used for feeding horses and decoration.
  • New Blocks and Items: Carpets and decorative blocks added more creative options.

This update made traveling the Minecraft world way cooler and more stylish.
