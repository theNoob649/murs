Minecraft 1.14: Village & Pillage

Release Date: April 23, 2019

The Village & Pillage update completely revamped villages, adding new designs for different biomes and more dynamic villager behavior.

New mobs introduced:

  • Pillagers: Hostile mobs that attack villages with crossbows.
  • Ravagers: Large, tough mobs that serve as powerful attackers during raids.
  • Wandering Traders: Traders that roam and offer rare items.

Other highlights included new blocks like the Bell, Loom, and Campfire, as well as the introduction of raids—a new village defense challenge.
