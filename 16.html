Minecraft 1.16: The Nether Update

Release Date: June 23, 2020

The Nether got a serious glow-up in 1.16 with new biomes like the Crimson Forest and Warped Forest, plus awesome mobs like Piglins and Hoglins shaking up Nether survival.

Tiny addition:

Netherite, the new top-tier material, was quietly introduced, giving players the ultimate upgrade over diamond gear.
