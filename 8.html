Minecraft 1.8: The Bountiful Update

Release Date: September 2, 2014

The Bountiful Update expanded Minecraft's world and building systems significantly.

  • New Blocks: Slime blocks, prismarine, sea lanterns, granite, diorite, and andesite allowed for more creative builds.
  • Ocean Monuments: Massive underwater structures guarded by Elder Guardians, containing sponges and gold blocks.
  • New Mobs: Guardians and Elder Guardians, hostile fish-like creatures defending ocean monuments.
  • Banners: Players could craft and design banners for decoration and identity.
  • Rabbits: A small, neutral mob that drops rabbit hides and meat.
  • New Command Features: Armor stands, updated /execute commands, and customizable world generation gave mapmakers more tools than ever before.

Overall, 1.8 was a massive quality-of-life update that quietly shaped modern Minecraft’s structure and building style.
