Minecraft 1.18: Caves & Cliffs Part II

Release Date: November 30, 2021

The Caves & Cliffs Part II update revamped Minecraft’s terrain generation, creating massive mountains and sprawling caves that reshaped the world’s exploration and building experience.

Tiny addition:

Improved world height and depth limits quietly enhanced vertical gameplay, giving players new vertical space to conquer.
