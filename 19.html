Minecraft 1.19: The Wild Update

Release Date: June 7, 2022

The Wild Update took Minecraft back to nature with new biomes like the eerie Deep Dark and the lush Mangrove Swamps. It introduced the terrifying Warden mob and revamped frogs and tadpoles for more wildlife vibes.

Tiny addition:

New music tracks and ancient cities were quietly added, giving explorers fresh sounds and secrets to hunt.
