Bundles of Bravery

The Second Drop of 2024

  • Java: 1.21.2
  • Bedrock: 1.21.40
  • Released: October 22, 2024

The first game drop to land after Tricky Trials was a small one, but it settled one of the longest-running open questions in Minecraft history. Bundles had been announced back in 2020, pulled from release, and left in experimental limbo for years. Here they finally shipped.

  • Bundles: A container item that holds one stack worth of mixed items in a single slot — the answer to cluttered inventories. Craftable in all 16 dye colors.
  • Hardcore mode on Bedrock: One life, no respawn. Previously a Java-only mode, now available on Bedrock Edition and Realms.
  • Inventory and parity changes: A pile of smaller quality-of-life fixes bringing the two editions closer together.

Why it mattered: Bundles of Bravery proved the point of the drop model. Under the old annual-update cadence, a feature this small would have been held back for months to pad out a bigger release. Instead it shipped the moment it was ready.
