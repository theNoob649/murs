Minecraft 1.4: The Pretty Scary Update

Release Date: October 25, 2012

This update brought Halloween vibes to Minecraft with new mobs, items, and spooky features.

  • Witches: Hostile mobs that throw harmful potions.
  • Bats: Ambient flying mobs adding atmosphere to caves.
  • Wither Boss: A challenging new boss mob that drops Nether Stars.
  • Beacons: Powerful blocks that give players status effects in an area.
  • Carved Pumpkins: Decorative blocks usable to wear or build golems.
  • Anvils: Repair and rename items.
  • New Commands: Added to improve server and map maker tools.

The Pretty Scary Update balanced fun and fear, introducing one of Minecraft’s most iconic bosses and lots of useful new features.
