Minecraft 1.20: Trails & Tales

Release Date: June 7, 2023

Trails & Tales celebrated storytelling and customization. Players unearthed archeological sites with ancient pottery shards, crafted decorative pots, and explored cherry blossom biomes. New mobs like camels changed travel, while bamboo wood opened up fresh build styles.

Tiny addition:

Armor trims, added quietly mid-update, let players customize their gear’s look using rare templates and gemstones like diamonds, iron, gold, amethyst, netherite, etc.
