Minecraft 1.21: Tricky Trials

Release Date: June 13, 2024

The Tricky Trials update introduced Trial Chambers—randomized dungeon structures packed with traps, loot, and the new Breeze mob, known for its wind-powered attacks. Ominous Trials brought riskier challenges with greater rewards, while Vault loot expanded late-game gearing options.

This update turned Minecraft into a true dungeon crawler, with vault loot and ominous trials giving late-game players a reason to keep exploring.

It was also the end of an era. Tricky Trials was the last of the big annual updates. Everything that followed arrived as a game drop — smaller, named, themed releases shipping three to four times a year.

A common mix-up: the Happy Ghast (1.21.6), the Copper Golem and copper gear (1.21.9), and the Pale Garden (1.21.4) are not part of Tricky Trials. They share the 1.21.x version prefix, but each is its own separate drop with its own announcement and development cycle.
