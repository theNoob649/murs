Minecraft 1.21: Tricky Trials

Release Date: June 13, 2024

The Tricky Trials update introduced Trial Chambers—randomized dungeon structures packed with traps, loot, and the new Breeze mob, known for its wind-powered attacks. Ominous Trials brought riskier challenges with greater rewards, while Vault loot expanded late-game gearing options.

But Mojang didn’t stop at launch content:

  • Happy Ghast (added in 1.21.8): A new variant of the Ghast. The player must find a "Dried Ghast" in the Nether and place it in a water source block in the Overworld to spawn a Happy Ghast.
  • Copper Armor & Tools (added in 1.21.9): A stylish new gear tier, stronger than stone but weaker than iron.
  • Bugfix patches (1.21.1 – 1.21.7): Improved dungeon balance, Breeze AI, and vault loot drops.

This update turned Minecraft into a true dungeon crawler with loot and surprises constantly evolving in post-release patches.
