Chaos Cubed

The Second Drop of 2026

  • Java: 26.2
  • Bedrock: 26.30
  • Released: June 16, 2026

Announced at Minecraft Live in March 2026 and released three months later, Chaos Cubed took the underground somewhere it hadn't been: sulfur.

  • Sulfur springs and sulfur caves: A new underground biome built around volcanic vents.
  • Sulfur Cube: A new mob native to the sulfur caves.
  • Sulfur blocks and sulfur items: New material set.
  • Cinnabar: New ore and block.
  • New music disc.

Announcement to release: roughly three months. Under the old annual cadence, a feature revealed at Minecraft Live could be nearly a year away from players. The gap tightening like this is the clearest evidence of what the drop model actually changed.
