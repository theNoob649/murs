Minecraft 1.11: Exploration Update

Release Date: November 14, 2016

The Exploration Update expanded Minecraft’s sense of adventure.

Key features included:

  • Woodland Mansions: Rare, massive structures deep within dark forests, housing new mobs.
  • Illagers: Introduced the Evoker, Vindicator, and summoned creatures like the Vex.
  • Llamas: Tameable pack animals capable of forming caravans.
  • Explorer Maps: Cartographer villagers now sold maps leading to Woodland Mansions and Ocean Monuments.
  • Totem of Undying: A rare item that saves the player from death once when held.

This update made exploration more rewarding with both danger and treasure waiting in the game's remotest places.
