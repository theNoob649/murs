Minecraft Pre-Classic

Release Date: May 10, 2009

The very first phase of Minecraft’s development, Pre-Classic was a simple creative sandbox with limited blocks and basic terrain generation. There were no survival elements, no mobs, and no crafting — just raw building fun in a blocky world.

This early stage laid the foundation for what would become the iconic Minecraft experience, experimenting with infinite worlds and basic block placement mechanics.

Though primitive by today’s standards, Pre-Classic sparked the creative freedom that would define Minecraft’s future.
