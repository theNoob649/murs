Minecraft Alpha

Release Date: June 30, 2010

Alpha expanded Minecraft’s survival mechanics massively, introducing the Nether dimension, weather effects, pistons, enchanting, brewing, and hardcore mode.

Gameplay became deeper, with more mobs and new mechanics adding layers of complexity and fun.

  • Added Nether dimension and new biomes
  • Introduced weather and environmental effects
  • Added pistons, enchanting, and brewing systems
  • Hardcore mode for the ultimate survival challenge
