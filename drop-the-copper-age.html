The Copper Age

The Third Drop of 2025

  • Java: 1.21.9
  • Bedrock: 1.21.110
  • Released: September 30, 2025

Copper had been in Minecraft since 1.17 and had almost nothing to do. This drop fixed that in one pass, promoting it from a decorative curiosity to a full material tier with its own utility mob.

  • Copper Golem: A buildable utility mob that picks up items and sorts them into nearby chests. Like the iron golem, you construct it from blocks. Unlike the iron golem, it oxidizes over time and eventually freezes into a statue.
  • Copper tools, weapons, and armor: A complete gear tier sitting between stone and iron.
  • Copper storage and lighting: Copper chests, copper lanterns, copper torches, copper bars, and expanded grate and bulb families.
  • Copper golem statues: Decorative variants for each oxidation stage.
  • Shelf block: A new storage-display block.

The oxidation loop: A copper golem left alone slowly weathers and stops working — but you can wax it to freeze its state, or scrape it back with an axe. It's the first mob whose lifecycle is tied to a block mechanic, and it turned copper into the most mechanically interesting material in the game.
