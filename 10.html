Minecraft 1.10: Frostburn Update

Release Date: June 8, 2016

The Frostburn Update brought freezing biomes and fiery Nether expansions together in a diverse mix of content.

Main additions included:

  • Strays: Skeleton variants found in snowy biomes, firing arrows of Slowness.
  • Husks: Desert-dwelling zombie variants that inflict Hunger.
  • Polar Bears: Neutral mobs that roam icy areas, sometimes protecting cubs aggressively.
  • Magma Blocks: Hot, damaging blocks found in the Nether and ocean ravines.
  • Nether Wart Blocks & Red Nether Bricks: New decorative blocks to diversify Nether builds.
  • Auto-Jump: A new accessibility feature that lets players hop up blocks automatically.

Version 1.10 balanced frozen landscapes and fiery depths, subtly expanding survival challenges and build styles.
