Minecraft 1.0: Official Release

Release Date: November 18, 2011

Minecraft’s first full release marked the end of the Beta era and brought tons of new features and polish:

  • The End Dimension: Introduced a new realm featuring the Ender Dragon boss fight, a major Minecraft milestone.
  • Enchanting: Added enchanting tables to upgrade weapons, tools, and armor.
  • Potion Brewing: Brought brewing stands and potions for new gameplay strategies.
  • Experience Orbs: Players gained XP from mobs and mining to use for enchanting.
  • Hardcore Mode: A permadeath difficulty for the ultimate survival challenge.
  • New Mobs: Added witches and silverfish to keep the world dangerous.
  • Signs & Beds: Added for better player navigation and respawning.

This update was the official graduation from Beta to a complete game and laid the foundation for everything Minecraft is today.
