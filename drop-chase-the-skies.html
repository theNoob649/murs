Chase the Skies

The Second Drop of 2025

  • Java: 1.21.6
  • Bedrock: 1.21.90
  • Released: June 17, 2025

The drop that gave Minecraft its first real group flight. Chase the Skies took the Ghast — a mob that had spent thirteen years exclusively trying to kill you — and turned it into a shared airborne mount.

  • Happy Ghast: Find a dried ghast block in the Nether, bring it to the Overworld, and place it in water. It rehydrates into a Ghastling, which grows into a rideable, tameable Happy Ghast.
  • Harnesses: Equip a Happy Ghast with a harness (16 dye colors) and up to four players can ride it at once.
  • Locator bar: A HUD element showing where other players are relative to you — a major multiplayer navigation change.
  • Reworked leads: Multiple mobs on one lead, plus improved leash knot behavior.
  • Craftable saddles: Saddles finally got a recipe instead of being loot-only.
  • New music discs: "Tears" and "Lava Chicken."

Note for readers of our 1.21 article: the Happy Ghast is often mistaken for a Tricky Trials feature because it arrived during the 1.21.x version range. It isn't — it's the headline feature of its own drop, developed and announced separately.
