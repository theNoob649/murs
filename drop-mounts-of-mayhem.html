Mounts of Mayhem

The Fourth Drop of 2025 — and the End of an Era

  • Java: 1.21.11
  • Bedrock: 1.21.130
  • Released: December 9, 2025

A combat and traversal drop — and the last Minecraft release ever to use the classic 1.x.y version number.

  • Spear: A new weapon that works both as melee and as a throwable, filling the gap between sword and trident.
  • Nautilus: A rideable underwater mount, giving the ocean the equivalent of a horse for the first time.
  • Nautilus armor: Protective barding for your mount.
  • Netherite horse armor: The long-missing top tier of horse barding.
  • New zombie variants: Biome-specific zombie appearances.
  • Mounted combat balance: Reworked numbers to make fighting from a mount actually viable.

The last 1.x release. After Mounts of Mayhem, Mojang retired the version scheme Minecraft had used since 2011. The next drop shipped as 26.1. See How Game Drops Work for what the new numbers mean.
