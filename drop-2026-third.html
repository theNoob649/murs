The Third Drop of 2026

Unnamed — Currently in Snapshots

  • Java: 26.3
  • Bedrock: 26.50
  • Status: Not yet released — expected Q3 2026

Everything below is unreleased. These features are currently in snapshots and previews. Mojang changes, delays, and occasionally cancels snapshot features before release. Nothing here is final, including the absence of a drop name.

Announced at the bonus Minecraft Live show on May 30, 2026, this drop leans autumnal — a new forest biome, a new wood set, and an unusually large pile of soft furnishings.

  • Dappled Forest: An autumn biome with poplar trees in red, orange, and yellow leaf variants.
  • Poplar wood set: The full complement — logs, planks, stairs, slabs, doors, fences, boats.
  • Wool stairs and slabs: In all 16 colors, a long-requested building addition.
  • Cushions: Sittable entities in 16 colors.
  • Straw bed: Lets you sleep through the night but does not set your spawn point.
  • Abandoned Camp: A new structure generating across several biomes, with loot chests.
  • Red shrubs and shelf mushrooms: New flora — the mushrooms are bouncy.
  • /posteffect command: A technical addition for applying post-processing shaders.
