The Garden Awakens

The Third Drop of 2024

  • Java: 1.21.4
  • Bedrock: 1.21.50
  • Released: December 3, 2024

Minecraft's first genuinely unsettling biome. The Pale Garden is a bleached, colorless variant of the dark forest — no passive mobs, no ambient noise, just grey wood and pale moss. And something in it that watches you.

  • The Creaking: A hostile mob that only moves when no player is looking at it. It cannot be killed directly; you have to find and destroy the creaking heart that anchors it.
  • Creaking heart: The block that spawns and sustains a Creaking, hidden inside pale oak trunks.
  • Pale Garden biome: Pale moss carpet, hanging moss, and a permanently drained color palette.
  • Pale oak wood set: A full new wood type — the palest in the game.
  • Resin: Resin clumps, resin blocks, and resin bricks, bled from the creaking heart.
  • Eyeblossom: A flower that opens at night and closes during the day.

Design note: The Creaking is one of the few Minecraft mobs built around a mechanic rather than a stat block. It has no attack pattern to learn — the tension comes entirely from line of sight, which makes exploring the Pale Garden with friends genuinely different from exploring it alone.
