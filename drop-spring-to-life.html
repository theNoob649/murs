Spring to Life

The First Drop of 2025

  • Java: 1.21.5
  • Bedrock: 1.21.70
  • Released: March 25, 2025

An atmosphere drop. Spring to Life added almost nothing you can fight and almost nothing you can craft into gear — instead it went after the thing Minecraft biomes had always been weakest at: feeling like distinct places.

  • Mob variants: Cows, pigs, and chickens now come in warm, cold, and temperate variants that spawn to match their biome. The same animal looks different in a savanna than in a taiga.
  • Leaf litter and falling leaves: A new ground block plus drifting particle effects under trees.
  • New flora: Firefly bush, wildflowers, bush, cactus flower, and dry grass.
  • Ambient sound overhaul: Significantly expanded biome-specific audio, including new Pale Garden ambience.

The firefly bush is worth a special mention — it emits drifting light particles at night without being a light source itself. It was a direct nod to the fireflies Mojang had announced for The Wild Update in 2022 and then cancelled.
