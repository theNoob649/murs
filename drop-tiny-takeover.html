Tiny Takeover

The First Drop of 2026

  • Java: 26.1
  • Bedrock: 26.10
  • Released: March 24, 2026

The first release under Minecraft's new version numbering — and fittingly, a drop about small things. Tiny Takeover went through the game's baby mobs, most of which were just scaled-down adult models, and gave them real designs.

  • Baby mob overhaul: New models for baby animals across many species, replacing the old shrunk-adult approach.
  • Rabbit rework: Reworked models, new variants, and updated behavior.
  • Golden dandelion: A new flower.
  • Craftable name tags: Name tags got a recipe, ending their status as one of the last purely loot-gated utility items.

Reading the new version number: 26.1 means "2026, first drop." There is no more 1.x. Java and Bedrock share the year but their second number diverges — Bedrock ships more intermediate patches, so this same drop is 26.10 there.
