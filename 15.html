Minecraft 1.15: The Buzzy Bees Update

Release Date: December 10, 2019

This update brought bees buzzing into Minecraft with new bee mobs, beehives, and honey blocks, adding fresh mechanics around pollination and farming.

Tiny addition:

Honey blocks quietly became a game-changer for builders and redstone engineers thanks to their sticky and sliding properties.
